Our Interventions

Core Services

Pre & Post-Natal Health Expert

Why it's important: The physical journey of pregnancy and postpartum recovery is incredibly demanding. Without proper guidance, women often suffer from pelvic floor dysfunction, diastasis recti, and chronic back pain.

How we help: We provide specialized, clinically safe protocols to prepare your body for labor and aggressively rehabilitate your core and pelvic floor postpartum, ensuring you regain your strength and confidence.

Learn More About Women's Health →

Ergonomist

Why it's important: Most chronic neck, back, and shoulder pain stems from poor workspace setups and repetitive strain. Your environment dictates your posture.

How we help: As a certified ergonomist, Dr. Hardika Jain (PT) evaluates your daily mechanics and workspace setup. We treat the existing pain while implementing postural corrections and ergonomic modifications to prevent the pain from ever returning.

Certified Yoga Instructor

Why it's important: True healing requires nervous system regulation. Traditional clinical therapy often misses the mind-body connection, leading to stress-induced muscle tension.

How we help: By integrating medical knowledge with therapeutic yoga, we prescribe specific asanas and breathwork to down-regulate your nervous system, increase flexibility, and accelerate tissue healing.

NDT & PNF (Neuro-Rehabilitation)

Why it's important: Conditions like stroke, MS, and severe nerve injuries disrupt the brain's ability to communicate with muscles. Standard exercises aren't enough to rebuild these neural pathways.

How we help: Using highly advanced Neuro-Developmental Treatment (NDT) and Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation (PNF), we manually guide your body to stimulate the brain and nervous system. This forces neuroplasticity—literally rewiring your brain to regain motor control, balance, and functional independence.

Certified Manual Therapist Level 1

Why it's important: Joint stiffness, fascial restrictions, and deep muscle spasms cannot always be stretched or exercised away. They require precise, hands-on intervention.

How we help: We use specialized manual therapy techniques to mobilize restricted joints, break down scar tissue, and release deep muscle tension. This immediate pain relief opens the door for effective, pain-free movement therapy.
